FlashGrabber

The app to make custom flashcards from your screenshots.

The idea for this app came while studying web development. I had the need to use flashcards, but I didn't want to hand write them. Often I would be reading materials on my mobile device and would take a screen shot with the intention of looking at it later. Flashgrabber allows you to take that screenshot, translate it into text using OCR, and choose the words you want for each the question and the answer portions of your flashcard.

You can preview FlashGrabber here or on github .

Grapher

The app to graph your Strava data.

Grapher is a web app designed utilizing the Strava API to find your starred segments and provide additional information in the form of a graph. Strava is a fantastic tool, Grapher simply makes digging deeper into your personal data an easy task.

You can preview Grapher here or on github .

Learn Words

The App to learn new words in context.

Learn Words is a web app designed for those who want to expand their vocabulary. The database is preloaded with classic books, and when you want to learn a new word, a paragraph from one of these books that contains your word, appears on screen. This is accomplished using Django and beautifulsoup4.

You can preview Learn Words here or on github .
